Why is Global Warming Bad?

Global Warming is Bad Because:

  • Droughts, intense storms, and floods will happen more. There will be more evaporation because of the higher temperature.
    • https://www.climatecommunication.org/climate/global-warming/#:~:text=Global%20warming%20is%20increasing%20th...
  • Lakes and ponds will get bigger because of the floods.
  • There will be more mosquitoes, fleas, and ticks because of the warmer weather. They can carry diseases.
  • The rising temperatures will cause more air pollution. It will also increase airborne pollen, which is bad for those who have allargies.
  • Many animals will become extinct. If they don't adapt quickly, they will not be able to survive the warmer weather.
    • https://www.nrdc.org/stories/are-effects-global-warming-really-bad

Animals That Might Go Extinct Because of Global Warming:

  • Bumblebees
  • Whales
  • Asian elephants
  • Giraffes
  • Insects
  • Ocean birds
  • Sharks
  • Coral reefs
  • Monarch butterflies
  • Great apes
https://www.earthday.org/how-climate-change-is-threatening-our-species/

These are only a few animals that are endangered. There are many more animals that might become extinct because of climate change.
