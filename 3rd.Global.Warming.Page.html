Good News! There's a Solution!

This global warming sounds like it's too big of a problem to solve, right?

It sounds so bad, people all over the world are probably hiding under their beds with a million electric fans, waiting for doom to drop on their heads.

Well, good news!

Nobody's doing that!(As far as I know.)

Why would they when there's a solution?

There are ways you can try to stop climate change.

Here is a list of ways you can help stop global warming:

  • Renewable energy. Renewable energy is energy that would never run out. You can get solar panels or something. Solar panels turn sunshine into energy, though they are expensive.
  • Use less air conditioning. Air conditioning uses up a lot of energy.
  • When you need a new refrigerator, washing machine, or other machine, look for one with an energy star label. It will tell you which ones waste the least amount of energy.
  • Waste less water. It takes a lot of energy to pump, heat, and treat your water.
  • Replace your lightbulbs with better ones. LED lightbulbs are okay.
  • Don't keep a computer, phone, or other device charging. Pull out the plug when you don't need it.
  • Use a car that doesn't use gasoline. Electric cars can charge using an outlet.
  • Keep your car tires inflated the right way. You could waste less gas that way. Also, if you gett a new air filter, that helps too.
  • Live in a city where you can walk to your destination. Planes, cars, and trains all release greenhouse gases. Trains are better than planes, though.
  • Tell your friends and family to help stop global warming.
